üöÄ Phred Platform Features

Phred is a comprehensive defensive phishing simulation platform designed to help organizations test and improve employee security awareness through realistic, measurable training campaigns.

üìß Campaign Management

  • Template Library: Create and manage reusable phishing email templates with variable substitution
  • Target Segmentation: Organize employees by department, role, risk level, or custom groups
  • Campaign Scheduling: Schedule campaigns for specific dates and times with automated execution
  • Multi-Stage Campaigns: Design sophisticated spear phishing simulations with follow-up emails
  • SMTP Integration: Connect to your email server with TLS/SSL, DKIM signing, and authentication

üìä Real-Time Tracking & Analytics

  • Email Open Tracking: 1x1 pixel tracking to detect when emails are opened
  • Link Click Tracking: Monitor which users click phishing links with unique tracking URLs
  • Form Submission Tracking: Track credential entry and form interactions (credentials not stored)
  • Keystroke Monitoring: JavaScript-based tracking captures user interactions on phishing pages
  • Real-Time Dashboard: Live metrics, activity feeds, and instant alerts for high-risk interactions
  • Department Breakdowns: Analyze vulnerability by department, role, or employee group

üéØ Advanced Features

  • Employee Segmentation: Create custom groups for targeted training based on risk profiles
  • Positive Reinforcement: Reward employees who report suspicious emails with immediate feedback
  • Response Monitoring: Track email replies and multi-stage conversation simulations
  • Dwell Time Analysis: Measure time between email delivery and first report
  • Immediate Training: Automatically redirect users to security awareness training after interaction
  • Custom Landing Pages: Design realistic phishing pages that match your organization's look

üîê Security & Compliance

  • DKIM Email Signing: Authenticate emails with DomainKeys Identified Mail for legitimacy
  • SPF/DMARC Support: Configure email authentication records for better deliverability
  • Rate Limiting: Throttle email sending to avoid spam detection and maintain reputation
  • Secure Credential Handling: Form submissions are logged for tracking only, credentials are never stored
  • API Key Authentication: Secure REST API for callback agents and integrations

üìà Reporting & Insights

  • Comprehensive Reports: Generate detailed campaign reports with metrics and recommendations
  • CSV Export: Export campaign data for further analysis in Excel or BI tools
  • Time-Based Analysis: Track campaign performance over time with historical data
  • Security Recommendations: Get actionable insights to improve your security posture
  • Training Effectiveness Metrics: Measure improvement in employee awareness over time

üîå Integrations & Automation

  • Email Provider OAuth: Connect Gmail, Outlook, and other providers for automatic reply monitoring
  • Callback Agent System: Deploy tracking agents (local or via proxies) for real-time event collection
  • JavaScript Tracking: Embed lightweight tracking scripts in phishing pages for detailed interaction capture
  • REST API: Integrate with existing security tools and SIEM systems
  • LLM Integration: Use AI to draft realistic phishing emails with EgoLlama Gateway

üí° Why Phred?

Phred transforms security awareness training from a checkbox exercise into a measurable, data-driven program. By simulating real-world phishing attacks in a controlled environment, organizations can identify vulnerabilities, measure employee awareness, and provide targeted training that actually improves security posture. With real-time tracking, comprehensive analytics, and automated training delivery, Phred makes security awareness training both effective and efficient.
